## What is Paytm and How Does It Work?

Paytm (Pay Through Mobile) started as a simple mobile recharge platform but has rapidly evolved into India's premier multi-service fintech ecosystem. At its core, Paytm is a secure digital wallet and UPI (Unified Payments Interface) app that allows you to make instant, secure payments directly from your bank account.

Key features of the platform include:

*   **Instant UPI Transfers:** Send and receive money directly using a UPI ID, bank account details, or a phone number.
*   **Paytm Wallet:** A secure digital vault to store money for lightning-fast, one-tap payments without entering a UPI PIN every time.
*   **Utility Bill Payments:** Pay electricity, water, gas, broadband, and DTH bills in seconds.
*   **Travel & Entertainment Bookings:** Book flights, trains, buses, and movie tickets directly inside the app.
*   **Financial Services:** Access mutual funds, digital gold, insurance, and personal loans all under one roof.
