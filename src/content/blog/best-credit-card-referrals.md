Credit cards are one of the most profitable financial products for banks. Because the lifetime value of a credit card user is high, card issuers and fintech platforms are willing to pay substantial commissions to acquire new customers. In India, referring a friend for a credit card can earn you anywhere from ₹500 to ₹3,000 per successful approval.

In this guide, we review the top credit card referral programs in India and explain how you can start earning passive income by sharing your invite links.

---

### Key Benefits of Credit Card Referrals

Unlike standard digital wallets or UPI apps that offer small ₹20–₹100 bonuses, credit card referrals pay out much higher rewards:
*   **Direct Bank/Statement Credits:** Rewards are credited as statement balances, reducing your monthly credit card bill.
*   **Premium Brand Vouchers:** Vouchers for Amazon, Flipkart, or Taj Hotels.
*   **Sign-Up Perks for Friends:** Referred friends often get first-year annual fee waivers or sign-up reward points.

### Top Credit Card Referral Programs in India

Here are the most popular and high-paying credit card invite programs currently active.

### 1. SBI Card Referral Program
State Bank of India (SBI) Card has a structured referral program called "Refer and Earn".

*   **Your Reward:** ₹500 worth of e-gift vouchers (Amazon, Flipkart, etc.) per approved card. You can refer up to 10 friends per year, earning up to ₹5,000.
*   **Friend's Reward:** ₹500 welcome voucher after paying the joining fee and making their first transaction.
*   **Best Cards to Refer:** SBI SimplyCLICK or SBI Cashback card.

### 2. HDFC Bank Credit Card Referrals
HDFC Bank runs seasonal invite campaigns for their popular cards through their official portal or mobile app.

*   **Your Reward:** Up to ₹1,500 Amazon voucher or cash credit.
*   **Friend's Reward:** Lifetime Free (LTF) card offer or bonus reward points.
*   **Best Cards to Refer:** HDFC Millennia or HDFC Regalia Gold.

### 3. Amex (American Express) Referral Program
Amex is known globally for having one of the most rewarding referral programs for premium cardholders.

*   **Your Reward:** Up to 8,000 Amex Membership Reward (MR) points (convertible to flights or hotels) per approved application.
*   **Friend's Reward:** First-year fee waiver or bonus MR points upon spending a minimum amount.
*   **Best Cards to Refer:** Amex Gold Card or Amex Platinum Travel Card.

### Tips for Successful Referrals and Approval

To make sure your referral is successful and the card is approved:
*   **Match the Credit Score:** Ensure the friend you invite has a good credit score (typically CIBIL score > 750) and a stable income. Banks reject applications with low credit history, and rejected applications do not earn referral bonuses.
*   **Apply via Link Only:** The applicant must complete the entire process directly via the referral link. If they exit the browser and apply later via the bank's main website, the link tracking will fail.
*   **Follow Up on Verification:** The bank will conduct a physical or digital video KYC. Remind your friend to complete this step promptly to avoid the application being marked as incomplete.
